%y https://youtu.be/DlkzYfxq4iM

%y https://youtu.be/TwSWSdM_6wM

[Save Glitch](./movement-and-glitches#glitches-save-glitch) during the ending dialogue of 05 to start chamber 06 outside of the elevator.
